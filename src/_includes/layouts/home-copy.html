Vinyl Tiles

{% image "./src/images/tile-thumb-1.jpg", "vinyl tile 2", "" %} {% image "./src/images/tile-thumb-2.jpg", "vinyl tile 3", "" %} {% image "./src/images/tile-thumb-3.jpg", "vinyl tile 4", "" %}

Luxury vinyl tiles are manufactured to create a textured surface, replicating the natural beauty of stone and wood as well as the elegance of marble and ceramics.

Unlike some natural products, vinyl flooring is quiet and warm underfoot, making it the perfect product for a lounge, bedroom or upstairs flat.

With high moisture-resistance, luxury vinyl tiles will not warp out of shape, making them a great choice in wet areas like kitchens and bathrooms.
